# Fair Usage Policy (FUP)

## Data fair usage

Plans advertised as "unlimited data" are subject to a fair-usage data cap, after
which the connection is throttled (not disconnected) to 1 Mbps for the rest of
the cycle. The cap depends on the plan:

| Plan | Daily FUP | Monthly FUP |
|---|---|---|
| LITE_299 | 1.5 GB | 30 GB |
| SMART_199 | 2 GB | 40 GB |
| PRO_599 | 3 GB | 75 GB |
| MAX_999 | 5 GB | 150 GB |

Hotspot / tethering usage counts against these limits.

## Voice and SMS fair usage

"Unlimited" voice and SMS quotas are capped at 250 minutes/day and 100
SMS/day respectively, intended for personal use. Bulk SMS, robocall, or
commercial use can trigger temporary suspension pending review.

## Roaming data

International roaming data is billed separately from domestic plans. Roaming
add-ons (see `ROAM_INTL_7D_499`) include a hard cap of 500 MB/day, after
which data is suspended for the day to prevent bill shock.

## Throttling restoration

Throttled speeds reset at midnight IST at the start of each new billing day.
Customers can purchase a top-up data add-on (`DATA_5GB_99`, `DATA_20GB_249`)
to restore full speeds for the remainder of the cycle.
