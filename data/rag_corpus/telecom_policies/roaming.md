# Roaming Policy (Domestic and International)

## National roaming

National (intra-India) roaming is free on all postpaid plans. Prepaid plans
include free national roaming for voice and SMS; outgoing data follows the
plan's standard quota.

## International roaming activation

International roaming is OFF by default and must be explicitly activated:
- via the app: Settings → International Roaming → Enable
- via the IVR: dial *121*5#
- in-store with a Govt-issued photo ID

Postpaid customers must have a clean payment history (no overdue bills) and
may need to provide a security deposit (₹2,500 default, refunded after 6
months of timely payment) before international roaming is enabled.

## International rates (without an add-on)

Pay-as-you-go rates apply only if no roaming add-on is active. Sample
indicative rates per destination zone (subject to change):

| Zone | Incoming call | Outgoing local | Outgoing to India | SMS | Data per MB |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Zone 1 (US, UK, Singapore) | ₹50/min | ₹85/min | ₹100/min | ₹15 | ₹350 |
| Zone 2 (EU, Australia) | ₹65/min | ₹110/min | ₹130/min | ₹15 | ₹400 |
| Zone 3 (Middle East) | ₹45/min | ₹80/min | ₹90/min | ₹15 | ₹300 |
| Zone 4 (rest of world) | ₹95/min | ₹150/min | ₹175/min | ₹20 | ₹600 |

## Recommended add-on

`ROAM_INTL_7D_499` (₹499 / 7 days) — 500 MB data + 100 minutes outgoing
(local or to India) + unlimited incoming + 50 SMS. Caps reset daily; unused
quota does not carry over.

## Bill shock protection

Per regulator guidelines we send an SMS at 50%, 80% and 100% of the daily
roaming cap, and suspend roaming data automatically once the daily cap is
reached. Suspension lifts at midnight local time of the visited country.
