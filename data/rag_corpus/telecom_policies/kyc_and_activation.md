# KYC and Activation Requirements

## eKYC documents

For new connections (prepaid or postpaid), the customer must complete eKYC
with one of:

- Aadhaar (with consent for OTP-based verification)
- Passport + recent utility bill (not older than 3 months)
- Driver's licence + Form 60 (if no PAN)

Self-attested copies are no longer accepted; the original must be presented
at activation, or scanned via the app's eKYC flow.

## SIM activation timeline

- Prepaid SIMs activate within 4 hours of successful eKYC.
- Postpaid SIMs activate within 24 hours after a soft credit check is
  cleared. Credit checks are run automatically against the customer's PAN.

## Re-KYC

Existing customers are required to complete re-KYC every 5 years, or
immediately when:
- Government-issued ID has expired
- The customer changes registered address
- A SIM has been replaced (lost/stolen) — a fresh KYC is mandatory before
  the new SIM activates

## Minors

Connections in the name of customers under 18 are not permitted. A parent
or legal guardian must be the registered subscriber until the customer
reaches 18; the SIM may then be transferred via a transfer-of-ownership
request at a store.
