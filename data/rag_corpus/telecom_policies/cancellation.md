# Cancellation and Plan Termination Policy

## Postpaid plans

Postpaid customers may cancel their plan at any time. The following rules apply:

- A written cancellation request (via the app, email to support@telco.example,
  or in-store) is required. Phone cancellations are accepted only after
  identity verification.
- Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle. The
  customer remains liable for usage charges incurred until the effective date.
- A final bill is generated within 7 days of the effective cancellation date
  and is due within 14 days of issue.
- If the customer cancels within 14 days of activation, they are eligible for
  a full refund of the activation fee (₹199) and any unused portion of the
  monthly fee, prorated to the day.
- Cancellation requested mid-cycle for plans bundled with a hardware
  installment is subject to early-termination fees equal to the remaining
  installment balance.

## Prepaid plans

Prepaid plans cannot be "cancelled" in the postpaid sense — they simply expire
when the validity period ends. Unused balance is non-refundable after 90 days
of account inactivity, after which the account is closed and the mobile
number returns to the pool.

## Number portability

Customers cancelling postpaid plans may request a Unique Porting Code (UPC)
to take their number to another carrier. The UPC is valid for 4 days from
issuance. Outstanding dues must be cleared before the UPC is generated.

## Refund window

Refunds (including pro-rated monthly fee and unused add-on balance) are
processed to the original payment method within 7 business days of the
cancellation effective date.
